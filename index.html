About Us.

Canine Coffee is Seahams go-to coffee shop for dogs and their humans.

We have an extensive range of hot and cold drinks, delicious sweet treats for humans, and tasty snacks for their pups. Our special dog menu contains only the highest quality, natural ingredients that are both healthy and tasty.

Events.

We hold a range of seasonal events (follow us on social media to stay up to date) and would love to play host if you want to throw your pooch a birthday party or other social gathering!

If you'd like to get in touch about booking an event, please send us a message via our enquires page.
